--- title: "Arcadia" linkOn: "off" excerpt: " "Arcadia" is an interactive narrative app for the Arcadia story in Disney Imagination 2021. Users could seamlessly switch between the perspectives of a visitor and a designer. They can interact with the scene and enjoy interactive feedback as the story develops, while also having a whole picture of Acadia world. It offers a more intuitive and immersive experience of the venue's design, construction, and narrative flow. " img: "../images/portfolio/Acadia/Arcadia_Intro.jpg" intro: "
  • It utilizes multimedia elements, including text, sound, animations, special effects, etc.. to enhance the storytelling experience.
  • This app helps with storytelling of Arcadia, which is inspired by the classical literary work "The Peach Blossom Spring" -- Set in a future society, the "Peach Blossom Plan" attempts to create an ideal home with the bio-structure "Peach Blossom Giant Tree" and advanced AI "Kirin." However, this artificial utopia conceals unresolved crises with a disrupted balance between technology and nature. The protagonist, Ling, accidentally enters this tech-enriched peach grove and ventures into the inner world of "Kirin," embarking on a fantastical journey to unveil the hidden truths of the society.
